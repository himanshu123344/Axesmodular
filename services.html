FALSE CEILING

False ceiling services can play a significant role in both modular design and interior design by enhancing the aesthetics, functionality, and overall appeal of spaces. Integration of Services like Modular designs often involve integrating various services like lighting, HVAC, and wiring. False ceilings can conceal these services while allowing easy access for maintenance and repairs. Our Designing false ceilings with unique shapes, patterns, or materials can serve as focal points within a room, drawing attention and adding character.



FLOORING

It is the most important part in designing every space. Flooring decides that the space we are going to design is going to be darker or lighter. Every other element we incorporate in any room is based on the color and texture of the flooring. From Tiles to natural stones and engineered wood flooring we cover everything, both for interiors and exteriors.
